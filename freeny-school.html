Freeny Driving School

Built with Django, Django Rest Framework, TailwindCSS, FlowbiteCSS, and Amazon S3 Cloud Storage.

A full service online driving school that allows you to create an account and manage enrolled courses. Enroll, pay, and manage courses and profiles online. Easily view course documents and curriculum all in one place.
